Recurring Messages(3) - LynxBot manual

DESCRIPTION

Recurring Messages are messages automatically posted in the chat at a given interval.

There can be a maximum of five recurring messages. The interval at which they are posted must be a multiple of 5 minutes and no longer than 60.

USAGE

Recurring messages can be managed through the addrec and delrec commands, viewed using listrec, and enabled or disabled using setrec.

Recurring messages are stored in the recurring section of the settings file.
