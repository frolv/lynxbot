Introduction

LynxBot is a Twitch.tv IRC bot designed specifically for use on Old School Runescape streams. It offers a number of commands that assist Runescape streams, such as Grand Exchange and Hiscore lookups, as well as the flexibility of customization through user defined commands, automated responses and more.

Setup

In order to use this bot, you will have to create a new account on Twitch on which to run it.

After creating the account for your bot, visit twitchapps to generate an oauth token for it. This is the password your bot will use to login to Twitch IRC.

Open up settings.txt, and enter the name of your bot's Twitch account, the channel you want to join (typically your Twitch username prepended with a #) and the oauth token you generated (including the oauth:) in their respective fields.

You can now launch the bot and it will join your channel, ready for use.

For more detailed setup instructions, see the wiki.
